Life Expectancy over the Years

The life expectancy for the listed countries on the graph has been increasing over the years except Japan which had a downfall during the year 1945 due to the world war two impact.

With the advances in Modern Medicine and better awareness of health among people, has contributed heavily to the increase in Life Expectancy.

The Effect of Health Expenditure Allocation On Life Expectancy

It is crystal clear that health expenditure per capita has linearly related to life expectancy.

The third world countries allocate less for health expenditure per capita, which is one of the reasons why their life expectancy sits at the bottom of the table.

Does Women Live Longer ?

The life expectancy of women is much higher than that of Men across all countries. The discrepancy is varied across nations, though.

The difference in the lifestyle of both genders is one of the reasons why Women tops life expectancy index.
